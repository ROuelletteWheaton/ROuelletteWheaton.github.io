About Me

My name is Ryan Ouellette. I'm a junior at Wheaton College, majoring in history with a concentration in European history. My longtime love of making stuff is what led me to get into 3D printing, and that intrest brought me to this class. Other hobbies that I enjoy are Lego and model making, photography, writing and biking. History is my main passion, and I'm a regular volunteer at the American Heritage Museum in Hudson, MA, helping to educate people on and care for the collection of artifacts and vehicles, and sometimes get to ride them too! Hands-on history is incredibly useful for helping people conncet with history, and I'm happy to be a part of it.
